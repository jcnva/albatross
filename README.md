# Albatross
Userscript to filter ebird barcharts against your personal eBird lifelist, sort species by frequency, and chart Lifer Potential by Week. 

## eBird Life List in .CSV format
  1. Sign in to eBird
  2. Go to https://ebird.org/lifelist
  3. Click the “Download (csv)” button in the upper right
    
## Exclusions

* Excludes birds categorized as exotic escapees (category 'X'), hybrids, slashes, and spuhs.
